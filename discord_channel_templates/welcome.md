# Welcome to Sweetwater Bay

**An investigative horror experience powered by Claude AI.**

You're a researcher studying a remote coastal ecosystem where impossible things happen.

## How to Play

Type `!start` in #sweetwater-bay to create your private game thread. Then simply type what you want to do - no dice, no stats, just natural conversation. Your game auto-saves between sessions.

## What You Should Know

- This is single-player horror focused on investigation and moral choices
- Your decisions have real consequences
- Only you and the server owner can see your thread
- Each message costs API fees - please play respectfully
